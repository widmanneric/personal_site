  “It is possible for every phenomenological concept and proposition drawn from genuine origins to degenerate when communicated as a statement. It gets circulated in a vacuous fashion, loses its autochthony and becomes a free-floating thesis. Even in the concrete work of phenomenology lurks possible inflexibility and the inability to grip what was originally grasped.” -Heidegger

  I know of myself traits, I can approximate some rough image, a loosely bound scaffolding that is present as I drift through. Something of me is not artificial, some solid rod props me into the wind as a sail and lets loose the bits in between to be in contact with the current. Taken by moods to various junctures, thin mountain peaks and dewy valleys, every other bearing there between, dazed sungazing, sleepless nights, I’m set forth here and forever. Sooner I am with a flowing spirit, one captured in a treacherous place, sooner I come to be that which I am. Sooner does the external world become and pass through without resistance. The brick wall beyond my window passable, the thunderous collisions of the sky heard, the bleeding heart left to roll down the hill passionately. I am not a scarecrow on a stick, I am haunted, I feel significant, I feel an importance, superficial existentialism doesn’t define me because I assert myself.

  I will the way, living now one can live forever, pushing the center of my fabric and embracing the warm tenderness of its monolith. There’s nothing to forgive because there is nothing to grasp, there is only grasping of the phenomena caprice. I love the existence that consumes me and which I come forth within. I am an appendage dangling from that monolith. Fen ferns dazzle, a clump of stew in the bog rising with mud and a burning star.
